4.1 Deformation Of Solids

4.1.1 Hookes Law

Hooke's Law - The restoring force is directly proportional to the extension, provided the limit of proportionality is not exceeded

4.1.2 Elastic Deformation + Plastic Deformation

Elastic Deformationwhen the load is removed the material will return to its original length/shape. The material may or may not follow Hooke's law.
Plastic DeformationThe material is extendable but does not follow Hooke's law, any extenstion is permanent. <
